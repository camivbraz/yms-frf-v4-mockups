Cascade movement: when a vacancy opens at a lower site, the system moves vehicles already assigned to higher sites downward — all the way to the 1st Site. Each move still validates the destination site's individual configs (capacity, pools, Block Early Vehicles).

Vehicles waiting in the geofence area (no eligible site at check-in) are also re-evaluated following the 5→1 order. Once a vacancy opens at a site that passes all checks for that vehicle, it is assigned an entry tag and proceeds to queue check-in.
